About me

Hi, I’m Eugene Morozov, a former Automation Engineer turned aspiring Front-End Developer with a passion for creating dynamic, user-friendly web interfaces. With a decade of experience designing and optimizing complex automated systems, I’ve honed a unique blend of analytical problem-solving, technical precision, and creative design thinking.

During my journey in automation, I became proficient in coding, debugging, and troubleshooting systems—skills I’ve now seamlessly translated into web development. Through The Odin Project, I’ve acquired expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and modern front-end tools, along with a foundational understanding of full-stack development.

My background in PLC programming, SCADA systems, and HMI design gives me a strong foundation in logic, UI/UX design, and user-centered approaches. I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy bridging the gap between technical functionality and aesthetic design to deliver solutions that meet both user and business needs.

Currently based in Bali, Indonesia, I’m excited to bring my technical expertise, project management skills, and newfound passion for web development to a forward-thinking team. Let’s build something amazing together!
